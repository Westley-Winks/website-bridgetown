Who am I?

My name is Westley Winks. I try to live my life with integrity by living according to my core values:

For me, learning looks like <%= link_to 'reading books', 'collections/_pages/b.md' %>, <%= link_to 'coding', 'https://github.com/Westley-Winks' %>, solving puzzles, reading blog posts, <%= link_to 'writing', 'collections/_pages/p.md' %>, and trying new things. One of my weaknesses is learning for the sake of learning and staying on the theoretical side of things instead of applying them.

I enjoy being known as "the guy" who people call, knowing that I will put all my effort into helping them.
